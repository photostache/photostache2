    Faq

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Check out our FAQ's to check any of them answer your question, and if they don't feel free to send us an email!


    • Does Photostache require a deposit to rent a photo booth?

      A: Yes, we do require a deposit to rent a photo booth. A $100 non-refundable deposit ensures that our photobooth will be at your event.

    • Do you charge extra for delivery, setup, and breakdown of your photo booth?

      A: Setup and breakdown for Photostache Photobooths are never charged for. We do offer free delivery up to 40 miles from zip code 91803. There is a $0.50/mile charge for each mile over.

    • What city do you service?

      A: All of Los Angeles and Orange County. Free deilvery up to 40 miles from zip code 91803. There is a $0.50/mile charge for each mile over.

    • What is included in your hourly rate?

      A: Our basic package starts at a 2-Hour rental rate which includes: double prints of 4x6 or 2x6, friendly attendants, customized template, fun props, digital copies and setup/tear down.. For more information and details of our other packages and add-ons, please check the Packages section or Contact Us for package & pricing information.

    • Does Photostache Photobooth go to weddings?

      A: Of course! Our photobooth was designed for weddings as well as other events, such as birthday parties, corporate events, quinceaneras, bar/bat mitzvahs, etc. The photographs are the perfect party favor for your guests. A photobooth can create life long memories, provide entertainment and are so much fun.

    • What makes your photo booth different from other photo booth rentals?

      A: Here's the truth. We've been to many events where we've had the opportunity to snap our pictures using a photobooth. At times we were impressed with the quality of the photo and other times we were truly disappointed. We believe in quality. We want our customers to leave a lasting positive impression on our service and product. We want to capture a fun and memorable moment at your event. We utilize a DSLR camera, professional lighting, easy-to-use touch screen, dye-sub printer that prints in under 10 seconds, an abundance of fun props, as well as working closely with our clients to provide fully customized layouts to match their special event. We want you to end the night knowing that everything you got was well worth the research in finding the perfect photobooth for your event.

    • How much floor space is required?

      A: To be on the safe side, we require 9 ft. (long) x 9 ft. (wide) of space. We will also need table space to place our props on.

    • What type of electricity is needed?

      A: We require 1 dedicated 10 AMP outlet.

    • How long does it take for photos to be posted online?

      A: When you rent a photobooth from Photostache, your online gallery is posted within 48 hours of the event. Galleries can be viewed on Facebook under your permission and will also be e-mailed to you.

    • Can the color of the background be chosen?

      A: Yes, the background can be customized with any color. *Extra fees may be applied depending on the package chosen.

    • Does an attendant come with the photo booth rental?

      A: Yes, all of our photo booth rentals come with at least 2 friendly attendants.

    • Is there a limit to the number of photographs that can be taken?

      A: There is no limit on the number of photographs taken, although there is a limit on the number of prints printed depending on the package you choose.


    Still have questions? Send us an email! We would love to answer them! :D
